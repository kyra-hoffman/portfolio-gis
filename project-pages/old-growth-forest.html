Old Growth Forest Proposal
Project Details / Background
The Preserve at Vassar is in the process of establishing a designated section of forest to be protected as future old growth forest. I was tasked with mapping the boundaries of what will be designated as future Old Growth Forest. This proposal included areas of both the Preserve at Vassar and the main college campus. To generate the area, I used forest eco-region data on the landscape and cut out easements from utilities. This designation will protect these areas for generations to come and provide access to future grants.
