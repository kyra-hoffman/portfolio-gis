Project Details / Background
My senior thesis topic was about investigating the presence of beavers and how they interact with water quality and humans around them. It became a case study of the Casperkill stream in Poughkeepsie, NY. A culmination of my research was the creation of a suitability model for beaver presence along the Casperkill stream. This model was informed by both human-beaver and beaver-environment interactions. There were 5 criteria: vegetation, slope, distance from roads, land use, and width of riparian barrier.
