Land Use and New York State
As part of my final project for Cartography: Data Visualization with GIS, I explored the relationship between agricultural/urban land use and environmental factors across New York State.
Project Details / Background
Of my undergraduate coursework, I took two GIS courses. One of which was “Cartography: Data Visualization with GIS” which inspired me to continue with GIS in my research. This poster, “Land Use and its Effects on the Environment: New York State” was my final project for the course. The assignment was to use two different sets of data and a location that you connect with and use data visualization to show the story that the data presents. I chose to compare land use and environmental quality and find the relationship between the two in New York State cities.
