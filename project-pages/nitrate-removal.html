Nitrate Removal in Beaver Ponds
Project Details / Background
I built on my thesis research in the summer after graduating with Dr. Deon Knights in the Vassar College Earth Science Department. As part of this research, I have developed a model to upscale nitrate removal with multiple beaver ponds on a stream system. A schematic network of the stream was created using data from the USGS National Hydrology Database (NHD). Nutrient flux was calculated using equation 4 and 5 based on stream segment length and estimated uptake velocity (Knights et al. 2023). The estimated uptake velocity was calculated from the average stream channel uptake rates.

To explore the addition of beaver dams, nodes were added to the network to represent dams which allowed for further nutrient removal.
